Thank you to our Sponsor

Si Se Puede Foundation

The Si Se Puede Foundation has helped our team so much through our journey of NURC. The foundation has provided us with the materials and workspace we need to compete in this challenge. On top of this, they provided us with the kit which helped us finish our robot.The mentors at SSPF also helped us build and test our robot. Thank you SSPF for all the help you have given us.
